ABOUT ME

I love working with and analyzing data. My language of choice is R, but I also have experience with Python and Java. I have shared my love for tech with my community by co-running my school’s robotics team and programming team. Outside of high school, I have participated in various summer programs and worked on research at Brown. In 2018 I had the honor of being selected as 1 of 3 middle school students able to attend MIT’s prestigious Beaverworks program. Through this program, I was able to get a foothold in machine learning and, in 2019, won a hackathon for work on a natural language model. I have also attended Brown University’s summer program, where I studied data analytics. Using this knowledge, I am helping to research climate science at Brown. Additionally, I used my background in data analysis to win two prizes at HackMIT, MIT’s largest undergrad hackathon. This past summer, I took Econ S-10A, Principles of Economics: Microeconomics, virtually through Harvard’s Extension School.
